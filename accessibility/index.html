Accessibility

Accessibility at Prism Consulting

We are committed to making our website usable and understandable for everyone, including visitors who rely on assistive technologies or alternative input methods.

Last reviewed

May 20, 2024

Questions about this statement can be directed to our accessibility lead at hello@prismatic.consulting.

Our commitment

Prism Consulting designs and builds digital experiences so they remain perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for every visitor. Accessibility is part of our definition of quality, and we consider it at each stage of content creation, design, and engineering.

Standards we follow

We align our work with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA and apply the relevant success criteria across layout, color contrast, keyboard navigation, and compatibility with modern assistive technologies.

Testing and improvements

Our team continuously monitors accessibility through a mix of manual reviews, keyboard-only walkthroughs, screen reader spot checks, and automated audits using industry-standard tooling. We log findings, prioritize fixes, and track remediation through our regular delivery cadence.

Need help or found a barrier?

If you encounter an accessibility issue, request an alternative format, or have suggestions that would improve your experience, please contact us. Include as much detail as possible about the page, assistive technology, and the nature of the problem so we can reproduce and resolve it quickly.

Email our team at hello@prismatic.consulting. We aim to acknowledge all reports within two business days and provide updates on resolution progress.

Staying accountable

This statement will be reviewed at least annually or whenever major site updates are released. We document each review and refresh our roadmap to address new guidelines, tooling, and user feedback.
